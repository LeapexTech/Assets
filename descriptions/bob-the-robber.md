# Bob The Robber

## Overview
Bob The Robber is an adventure game where you help Bob achieve his goals through various missions. Solve puzzles, crack safes, and complete heists while avoiding security. The game combines stealth, puzzles, and strategy.

## Game Information

**Genre:** Adventure / Stealth

**Developer:** TinyDogGames

## Features
- Engaging gameplay
- Multiple levels and challenges
- Intuitive controls
- Fun and entertaining experience

## How to Play
Navigate through the game using your keyboard and mouse. Complete objectives, overcome challenges, and progress through various levels. Each level presents new opportunities to improve your skills.

## Platform Support

Bob The Robber can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.

## Why Play

Bob The Robber offers an engaging experience with dynamic gameplay and various challenges. Perfect for both casual gaming and competitive play, this game provides entertainment for players of all skill levels.
