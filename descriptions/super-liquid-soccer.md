# Super Liquid Soccer

## Overview

Super Liquid  Soccer is an engaging arcade-style 7-a-side football  game developed by Punyrobot, released on November 29, 2022. Designed for players who prioritize fun over intricate simulations, it offers fast-paced matches filled with dynamic gameplay, allowing users to execute chip passes, through balls, and powerful shots. The game features multiple teams composed of legendary players from various eras, providing a nostalgic yet fresh experience. Players can build and customize their dream teams, progressing from local amateur squads to top international teams, and compete in various tournament modes.

Super Liquid Soccer is available for PC and Mac users on Steam, ensuring accessibility for a wide range of players. With its emphasis on fluid mechanics, strategic team control, and a variety of game modes, it stands out as a must-play for fans of arcade-style sports games.

## How to Play

Navigate through the game using your keyboard and mouse. Complete objectives, overcome challenges, and progress through various levels. Each level presents new opportunities to improve your skills.

## Controls

- W, A, S, D to move
- Spacebar to pass
- J to shoot
- L to chip shot
- Q, E to switch players

## Platform Support

Super Liquid Soccer can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.
