# Uno

## Overview

UNO is a classic card game that has captivated players worldwide with its simple yet strategic gameplay. The objective is to be the first to score 500 points by matching cards by color or number and utilizing special action cards to disrupt opponents. These action cards include Skips, Reverses, Draw Twos, and Wild cards, each adding a unique twist to the game. The game is suitable for 2–10 players and is recommended for ages 7 and up.

In recent years, UNO has expanded its offerings with various editions and digital adaptations. The UNO Ultimate Edition, for instance, brings together the classic card game and all nine thrilling DLCs in one complete package, offering a variety of gameplay and customization options. Additionally, the UNO Show 'Em No Mercy DLC introduces new rules and action cards, providing a more competitive experience for players.

For fans of strategic card games, exploring titles like Clash Royale and BloonsTD 5 can offer similar engaging experiences. Additionally, if you enjoy games that require quick thinking and adaptability, Baldis Basics and Plants vs Brainrots might pique your interest.

## How to Play

Navigate through the game using your keyboard and mouse. Complete objectives, overcome challenges, and progress through various levels. Each level presents new opportunities to improve your skills.

## Controls

- Left Click to play card
- Space to draw card
- U to call "UNO"

## Platform Support

Uno can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.

Uno offers an engaging experience with dynamic gameplay and various challenges. Perfect for both casual gaming and competitive play, this game provides entertainment for players of all skill levels.
