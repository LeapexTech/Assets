## Overview

An engaging game experience.

## Game Information

**Genre:** Casual

## Features

- Engaging gameplay
- Multiple levels and challenges

## How to Play

Navigate through the game using your keyboard and mouse. Complete objectives, overcome challenges, and progress through various levels. Each level presents new opportunities to improve your skills.

## Controls

- Arrow keys to move
- Mouse to interact
- Spacebar to interact

## Platform Support

Jungle Mart can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.

## Why Play

Jungle Mart offers an engaging experience with dynamic gameplay and various challenges. Perfect for both casual gaming and competitive play, this game provides entertainment for players of all skill levels.
