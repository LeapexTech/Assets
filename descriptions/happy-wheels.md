# Happy Wheels

## Overview

Happy Wheels is a side-scrolling, physics-based obstacle course  game renowned for its dark humor and challenging gameplay. Players navigate through treacherous levels filled with deadly obstacles such as spikes, mines, and wrecking balls, all while controlling characters like Wheelchair Guy, Irresponsible Dad, and Segway Guy, each with unique vehicles and abilities.

In January 2024, the game received an update introducing two new levels (8 & 9) for Pogo Stick Guy, expanding the already diverse gameplay experience. Happy Wheels is available on multiple platforms, including web browsers, iOS, and Android devices, making it accessible to a wide audience.

Fans of Happy Wheels might also enjoy Ragdoll Archers, which offers similar physics-based challenges with a focus on archery. Additionally, Obby But Youre On A Bike provides obstacle course excitement with unique vehicular twists. For those interested in more physics-driven gameplay, Ragdoll Hit delivers engaging combat scenarios.

## Game Information

**Genre:** Casual

**Developer:** Jim Bonacci

## Features

- Engaging gameplay
- Multiple levels and challenges
- Intuitive controls

## How to Play

Navigate through the game using your keyboard and mouse. Complete objectives, overcome challenges, and progress through various levels. Each level presents new opportunities to improve your skills.

## Controls

- Arrow keys to move
- Spacebar to jump
- Z to eject from vehicle
- Shift or Ctrl for secondary actions
- Mouse to select menus or restart

## Platform Support

Happy Wheels can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.

## Why Play

Happy Wheels offers an engaging experience with dynamic gameplay and various challenges. Perfect for both casual gaming and competitive play, this game provides entertainment for players of all skill levels.
