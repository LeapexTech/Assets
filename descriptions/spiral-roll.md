# Spiral Roll is an engaging  arcade game that challenges players to carve spirals from wood using a chisel, aiming to destroy obstacles and progress through increasingly complex levels. The  game's intuitive tap-and-hold mechanics make it accessible to players of all ages, while its escalating difficulty ensures a satisfying experience for those seeking a challenge. As players master the art of creating perfect spirals, they can unlock new chisels and environments, adding variety and replayability to the gameplay.
