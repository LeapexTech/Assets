# Highway Traffic

## Overview

Highway Traffic turns navigating the highway into an exciting adventure. Buckle up and enjoy the thrilling ride! Navigate through busy roads, avoid obstacles, and reach your destination safely.

## Game Information

**Genre:** Racing / Casual

**Developer:** Unknown

## Features

- Dynamic highway driving
- Traffic navigation challenges
- Fast-paced action
- Skills-based gameplay
- Thrilling driving experience

## How to Play

Accelerate through highway traffic while avoiding collisions. Navigate between vehicles, dodge obstacles, and keep your cool while driving. The faster you go, the more exciting it becomes!

## Controls

- W / ⬆️ to accelerate
- A, D / ⬅️ ➡️ to steer
- SPACE to handbrake
- Left SHIFT / F for boost

## Platform Support

Highway Traffic can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems.

## Why Play Highway Traffic?

Highway Traffic offers an adrenaline-pumping driving experience where you must navigate busy roads with precision and quick reflexes. Perfect for those who love fast-paced, skill-based racing challenges.
