# Highway Traffic

## Overview

Highway Traffic turns navigating the highway into an exciting adventure. Buckle up and enjoy the thrilling ride! Navigate through busy roads, avoid obstacles, and reach your destination safely.

## Game Information

**Genre:** Racing / Casual

**Developer:** Unknown

## Features

- Dynamic highway driving
- Traffic navigation challenges
- Fast-paced action
- Skills-based gameplay
- Thrilling driving experience

## How to Play

Accelerate through highway traffic while avoiding collisions. Navigate between vehicles, dodge obstacles, and keep your cool while driving. The faster you go, the more exciting it becomes!

## Controls

- W / ⬆️ to accelerate
- A, D / ⬅️ ➡️ to steer
- SPACE to handbrake
- Left SHIFT / F for boost

## Platform Support

Highway Traffic can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.

## Why Play

Highway Traffic offers an engaging experience with dynamic gameplay and various challenges. Perfect for both casual gaming and competitive play, this game provides entertainment for players of all skill levels.
