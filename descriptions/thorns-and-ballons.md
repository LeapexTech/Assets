# Thorns And Ballons

## Overview

Thorn and Balloons is an engaging physics-based puzzle game that challenges players to pop all balloons in each level by launching a spiked ball with precise force and angle. As the thorn ball bounces off walls and obstacles, players must strategize to create chain reactions and clear the screen. With over 90 uniquely designed levels, the game progressively introduces moving platforms, spinning hazards, and other obstacles, enhancing the complexity and excitement.

For fans of physics-based puzzle games, Thorn and Balloons shares similarities with titles like BloonsTD 5, where strategic planning is key to popping balloons, and Blocky Puzzle, which also challenges players with intricate levels requiring precise movements. Additionally, if you enjoy games that test your aiming skills and involve bouncing mechanics, Gun Spin offers a unique experience in mastering trajectories.

## How to Play

Navigate through the game using your keyboard and mouse. Complete objectives, overcome challenges, and progress through various levels. Each level presents new opportunities to improve your skills.

## Controls

- Press and hold to aim
- Drag back to set power
- Release to launch
- Left Mouse Click to interact
- R to restart level
- P to pause game

## Platform Support

Thorns And Ballons can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.

Thorns And Ballons offers an engaging experience with dynamic gameplay and various challenges. Perfect for both casual gaming and competitive play, this game provides entertainment for players of all skill levels.
