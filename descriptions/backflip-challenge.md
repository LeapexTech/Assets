# Backflip Challenge

## Overview

Jump, flip, and splash your way to the finish! Play through fun levels, try cool characters, and do awesome flips on crazy courses. Bright cartoon graphics make every level exciting and fun to explore.

## Game Information

**Genre:** Casual

**Developer:** Unknown

## Features

- Fun flip-based mechanics
- Multiple character options
- Vibrant cartoon graphics
- Progressive difficulty levels
- Engaging level design

## How to Play

Navigate through levels by jumping and flipping to reach the finish line. Time your moves carefully to execute awesome flips and avoid obstacles. Collect items and complete objectives to progress.

## Controls

- Arrow keys to move
- Mouse to interact
- Spacebar to jump/flip

## Platform Support

Backflip Challenge can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern devices.

## Why Play Backflip Challenge?

Backflip Challenge offers a fun and engaging platforming experience with colorful graphics and creative level design. Perfect for casual gamers looking for entertaining challenges with a unique flip mechanic.
