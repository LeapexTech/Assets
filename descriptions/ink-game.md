# Squid Game 2 Mini Games

## Overview

Ink  Game is an immersive platformer that challenges players to navigate invisible terrains by splattering vibrant paint to reveal their surroundings. Each level requires defeating enemies and reaching the goal, with the added twist that the environment remains hidden until painted. Players can uncover the landscape by performing actions like double jumps, wall slides, or even by encountering obstacles, which release bursts of color to illuminate the path ahead.

For those who enjoy dynamic platformers with a creative twist, Ink Game offers a refreshing experience. Fans of titles like Obby But Youre On A Bike and Deadly Descent will appreciate the innovative mechanics and engaging challenges that Ink Game brings to the genre. Action& Platform Games

## How to Play

Navigate through the game using your keyboard and mouse. Complete objectives, overcome challenges, and progress through various levels. Each level presents new opportunities to improve your skills.

## Controls

- W, A, S, D to move
- Space to jump
- Left Shift to sprint
- Q to dash
- 1–5 to use items

## Platform Support

Squid Game 2 Mini Games can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.
