# Brookhaven

## Overview

Brookhaven is a popular role-playing game on the Roblox platform, offering players an expansive open-world experience in a modern suburban town. Since its launch in April 2020, it has attracted millions of users with its emphasis on creativity, social interaction, and immersive roleplay scenarios.

In Brookhaven, players can select and customize homes, ranging from cozy apartments to luxurious estates, and furnish them to reflect their personal style. The game provides a diverse array of vehicles, including cars, bikes, and even horses, enhancing the exploration of the town's various locations such as St. Lukes Hospital, Brookhaven School, and the Police Station.

Players can assume different roles, customize their avatars, and engage in various activities without the need for in-game currency. Recent updates have enhanced gameplay with features like the Horse Update, introducing new tools and props for creating obstacle courses, and the Arcade Update with interactive claw machines and bike racers.
