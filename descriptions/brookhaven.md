# Brookhaven is a popular role-playing  game on the Roblox platform, offering players an expansive open-world experience in a modern suburban town. ## Overview
In Brookhaven, players can select and customize homes, ranging from cozy apartments to luxurious estates, and furnish them to reflect their personal style. The game provides a diverse array of vehicles, including cars, bikes, and even horses, enhancing the exploration of the town's various locations such as St. Lukes Hospital, Brookhaven School, and the Police Station. Additionally, players can assume different roles, customize their avatars, and engage in various activities without the need for in-game currency.

Brookhaven has seen numerous updates that enrich the gameplay experience. For instance, the Horse Update introduced rebalanced horse speeds, new tools like the Horse Brush, and props for creating obstacle courses, adding depth to farm roleplay scenarios. Similarly, the Arcade Update revamped the in-game arcade with interactive elements such as claw machines and bike racers, providing new avenues for entertainment within the game.
