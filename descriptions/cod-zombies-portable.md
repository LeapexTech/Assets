# CoD Zombies: Portable (NZ:P) is a fan-made adaptation of the renowned Call of Duty Zombies mode, offering a retro horror experience reminiscent of Call of Duty: World at War. Built on a modified Quake engine, this  game challenges players to survive relentless waves of zombies, earning points to unlock new areas, acquire advanced weapons, and enhance their abilities through various perks. The arsenal includes iconic weapons like the Ray Gun and Wunderwaffe DG-2, and players can repair barriers to delay zombie incursions. With multiple maps inspired by the original series and support for online multiplayer co-op, NZ:P provides both solo and cooperative gameplay experiences.

## Overview

For enthusiasts of survival-based gameplay, Deadly Descent offers intense action as players navigate treacherous terrains, battling enemies and overcoming obstacles. Similarly, Backrooms immerses players in a mysterious environment filled with lurking dangers, testing their survival instincts and problem-solving skills. Fans of cooperative play might also enjoy Sh3llSh0ck, where teamwork is essential to overcome challenges and achieve objectives.

Since its release in October 2024, CoD Zombies: Portable has garnered attention for its nostalgic graphics and engaging mechanics. The game features customizable UI settings, including menu backgrounds and music, allowing players to tailor their experience. Each map presents unique layouts that influence  strategy, such as optimal camping spots and weapon spawn points, adding depth and replayability to the game.
