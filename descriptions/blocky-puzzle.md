# Blocky Puzzle

## Overview

Blocky  Puzzle is an engaging and challenging block  puzzle game that tests your strategic thinking and spatial planning skills. The objective is to drag and place various shaped blocks onto an 8x8 grid, aiming to fill rows or columns completely to clear them and earn points. The  game ends when there's no space left to place new blocks, encouraging players to plan their moves carefully to achieve high scores.

One of the standout features of Blocky Puzzle is its simplicity combined with depth. The game starts with straightforward levels that progressively become more complex, offering a satisfying challenge for both beginners and seasoned puzzle enthusiasts. Additionally, the game introduces a unique COMBO gameplay mechanic, where eliminating multiple rows or columns simultaneously triggers special animations and bonus points, adding an extra layer of excitement.

For players who enjoy strategic puzzle games, Blocky Puzzle shares similarities with titles like Grow A Garden, where planning and foresight are key to success. Additionally, fans of Idle Ants might appreciate the incremental challenge and satisfaction of optimizing their gameplay strategies.
