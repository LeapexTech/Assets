# Fast Food Rush

## Overview

Fast  Food Rush is a 3D  restaurant  game where you start small and grow your restaurant into something huge and popular!

## Game Information

**Genre:** Casual

**Developer:** Duck

## Features

- Engaging gameplay
- Multiple levels and challenges
- Intuitive controls
- Fun and entertaining experience

## How to Play

Navigate through the game using your keyboard and mouse. Complete objectives, overcome challenges, and progress through various levels. Each level presents new opportunities to improve your skills.

## Controls

- Move: WASD / ⬆️⬅️⬇️➡️/ Drag Mouse;
- Zoom in/out: Mouse Scroll;

## Platform Support

Fast Food Rush can be played on desktop browsers and is compatible with most modern systems including mobile devices.

## Why Play

Fast Food Rush offers an engaging experience with dynamic gameplay and various challenges. Perfect for both casual gaming and competitive play, this game provides entertainment for players of all skill levels.
